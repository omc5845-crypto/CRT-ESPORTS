RULES & REGULATIONS

📌 General Rules
  • All players must register before the deadline.
  • Each team must have exactly 4 players.
  • Players must use their registered BGMI UID during matches.
  • Tournament format: Battle Royale – Classic Erangel.
  • Match schedule will be shared via official channels.
  • Per squad entry fee: Rs.200 | 1st Prize: Rs.1400
  • Date: 17 May 2026 | Time: 5:00 PM – 8:00 PM
🚫 Prohibited Actions
  • Use of any third-party hacks, mods, or cheating software.
  • Teaming with opponents during the match.
  • Using emulators unless specifically allowed.
  • Abusive language or toxic behavior towards other players.
  • Stream sniping or any form of unfair advantage.
🎮 Match Rules
  • Players must be online 15 minutes before match start.
  • Room ID and password will be shared 10 minutes before match.
  • If disconnected, players may rejoin within match duration.
  • Scores calculated based on kills + placement points.
  • Admin decisions on disputes are final and binding.
⚠️ Important Notes
  • Registration fees are non-refundable after confirmation.
  • Prize distribution within 48 hours of tournament completion.
  • Any violation may result in immediate disqualification.
  • Screenshots/recordings may be required as proof.
  • Rules are subject to change — check for updates regularly.
🔴 IMPORTANT NOTE FROM ADMIN
  • Admin's Decisions will Be Final.
  • If Less Registrations, Tournament Will Be Postponed / Prize Pool Will Be Deducted.
  • More Tournaments will be organized — informed on our WhatsApp Group. Stay Tuned!
  • MONEY WILL NOT BE REFUNDED ONCE REGISTERED.
